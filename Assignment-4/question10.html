The Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is a majestic white marble mausoleum built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife, Mumtaz Mahal.

The Golden Temple

The Golden Temple, also known as Sri Harmandir Sahib, is a prominent Sikh Gurdwara located in Amritsar, Punjab, India.

Mysore Palace

Mysore Palace, also known as the Amba Vilas Palace, is a magnificent architectural gem located in Mysore, India.

The Lotus Temple

The Lotus Temple, situated in New Delhi, is an architectural marvel resembling a lotus flower, symbolizing unity, purity, and peace.

Qutub Minar

Qutub Minar is a UNESCO World Heritage site and the tallest brick minaret in the world, standing at a height of 72.5 meters.
